Common Questions

Is my money safe?

Yes. Relay uses Stripe's authorization system. When you post a job, the bank places a temporary hold on the funds. Relay initiates the transfer to the runner only after the job is marked complete.

How do you verify runners?

Runners must submit government-issued identification. We perform a check to ensure they are a real person with a legal presence in South Africa.

What if an item goes missing?

We verify identity and keep job telemetry (where permitted). If required by law, we can provide records to authorities. Because payment is authorized upfront and captured only after completion, you are not charged for failed deliveries.

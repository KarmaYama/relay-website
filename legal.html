1. Platform Nature

Relay ("The Platform") is a technology utility that connects independent Runners with Clients. Relay acts as a software platform and communication layer. Relay is not a transportation carrier, a logistics provider, or a bank.

2. Payments & Funds

Relay facilitates payments but does not hold user funds in a custodial capacity.

  • All payments are processed by Stripe via Connected Accounts.
  • When a job is posted, funds are "Authorized" (reserved) by the Client's bank.
  • Funds are "Captured" (charged and transferred) only upon confirmation of job completion.
  • If a job is cancelled before completion, the Authorization is voided; no charge is made.

Relay stores transaction references only (e.g., Payment Intent IDs), not card details.
